In reality, there are four real options we have for building nuclear reactors that are safe, minimize waste, and produce enough energy to take the nation off of fossil fuels. My personal favorite, and what I believe should be the future of energy production, is the Thorium reactor, read about it here.
The most common nuclear reactors are Pressurised Water Reactors, with 300 reactors spread across the world, first being used in submarines, and became a template for future designs, you can read about them here.
There's also Boiling water reactors, very similar in nature to PWRs, as well as Advanced gas cooled reactors, which use graphite moderators and CO2 as a primary coolant, different from the previous options we've discussed, it's covered here.
